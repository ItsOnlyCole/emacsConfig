---Centaur Tabs---
Next Tab: C-<Right>
Previous Tab: C-<Left>

---Multiple Cursors---
Add Cursor: C-S-<mouse-1>
Mark Next Like This: C->
Mark Previous Like This: C-<
Mark All Like This: C-c C-<
Mark Group of Lines: C-S-c C-S-c
New Lines: C-j
Unmark Lines: C-g

---Origami---
Start Origami: M-x origami-mode
Open Node: C-q
Close Node: C-w
Open Node & Child Nodes: C-S-q
Close Node & Childe Nodes: C-S-w

---Restart-Emacs---
m-x restart-emacs

---Shell Pop---
Open Shell: C-t

---Window Split---
Split Vertically: C-x 3
Split Horizontally: C-x 2
Show Only One Window: C-x 1

---Windmove---
Move to Left Window: C-c <Left>
Move to Right Window: C-c <Right>
Move to Above Window: C-c <Up>
Move to Below Window: C-c <Down>
